In the dangerous old wind area, a female boss's off-road vehicle broke down on the mountain road, blocking the way of the bus. The female boss refused to move the car. The wind and snow were getting heavier and heavier. In order to pass through the old wind outlet as quickly as possible, Hasimu had to take the risk of driving the bus close to the cliff and go around the off-road vehicle. The bus skidded and almost rolled down the cliff. Fortunately, Meng Beixi arrived in time and saved the lives of the entire vehicle. In order to avoid the upcoming heavy snowstorm, Meng Bei led everyone to move to a safer winter nest...
