Jiuzhaigou Valley, or Jiuzhaigou National Park is located in northern Sichuan province. 
<br/><br/>
It is known as “the Most Beautiful Waterscape in China”, Word Natural Heritage Site, “Fairytale Land”, 5A National Park. All people who have been to Jiuzhaigou know all these reputation match the reality. 
<br/><br/>
It is even regarded as the “Paradise for Photographers”.
<br/><br/>
Having been hidden as a mysterious and never been bothered by outsider until been found in the middle of 20th century, Jiuzhaigou keeps the wildness of nature very well. 
<br/><br/>
All the forests, lakes, waterfalls, landform are the best gifts from other mother nature.
